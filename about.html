For 55 years, Columbia Association has stewarded the open space, programs, and community infrastructure that make Columbia one of the most livable places in America — and we're just getting started.

What is CA?

Not a parks department.
Not a gym.
Something much more.

Columbia Association is a nonprofit community organization — but that description undersells what we actually do. We manage 43 facilities, 3,600 acres of open space, and a full calendar of community programs serving every stage of life.

We're funded by assessments on Columbia property — which means every dollar we generate goes back into the community we serve. No shareholders. No outside investors. Just Columbia.

The Columbia story

Wonder why Columbia is the way it is?

In 1967, developer James Rouse set out to build a city that proved people of all backgrounds could live well together — with open space protected by covenant, villages designed for human scale, and community infrastructure built into the city's DNA. Columbia Association was created to steward that vision. Everything CA does today traces back to that original intention.

Common questions

Things people
often wonder.

Is CA just a gym membership?
Not even close. CA manages 43 facilities across Columbia — pools, fitness clubs, tennis, pickleball, golf, an ice rink, an arts center, and 3,600 acres of open space. CA membership gives you access to all of it. Think of it less as a gym membership and more as a key to the full city.
I pay an assessment — does that mean I'm already a member?
Not automatically. If you own or rent on Columbia assessed property, your assessment funds CA — and it entitles you to a free Columbia Card, which gives you discounted rates on CA programs and significant savings on membership. But full membership, which unlocks all facilities and services, is a separate step. The Columbia Card is a great first step if you're not sure you're ready.
What's the difference between the Columbia Card and a CA membership?
The Columbia Card is free for any Columbia assessed-property resident — owner or renter. It gives you discounted access to CA programs and services, and a meaningful discount on full membership. CA membership unlocks everything — unlimited access to all 43 facilities, pools, fitness clubs, and the full range of programming. Columbia Card holders are the ones who typically go on to become CA members.
I don't live in Columbia — can I still access CA facilities?
Yes. CA memberships are available to non-residents too, though at a different rate than assessed-property residents. If you work in Columbia or live nearby, a CA membership can still be a great value. Visit the membership page to see all available options and current rates.

Community voice

This community
governs itself.

Columbia was designed with democratic governance built in. Each of Columbia's ten villages elects its own board representatives, and residents have a direct voice in how CA operates and invests. That's not a feature of a commercial gym — it's the structure of a community that takes itself seriously.

Village elections are open to all Columbia residents. Board meetings are public. If you live here, you have a say in how this community evolves.
